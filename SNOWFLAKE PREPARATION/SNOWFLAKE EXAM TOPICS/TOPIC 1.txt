Bulk unloading of data from Snowflake supports the use of a SELECT statement:

COPY INTO @my_stage/my_unloaded_data.csv
FROM (
SELECT col1, col2, col3
FROM my_table
WHERE condition
)
FILE_FORMAT = (FORMAT_NAME = 'CSV' COMPRESSION = 'AUTO');
